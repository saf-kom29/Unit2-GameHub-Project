Step 1: Add Google Font Link

Add this to your HTML head section! 
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
    <title>GameHub - Your Gaming Community</title>
    
    <!-- LESSON 9: Google Fonts -->

    <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">
</head>
Important: Google Font link must come BEFORE your CSS file!

Three font weights we're importing:

Step 2: Apply Font in CSS

Update your body font-family!

Font Weights Explained ‚öñÔ∏è

How thick or thin the text appears

100-300: Light (Thin)

400: Normal (Regular)

500: Medium

600: Semi-bold (Demi-bold)

700: Bold

800-900: Extra bold (Black)

For GameHub:
‚Ä¢ Body text: 400 (normal)
‚Ä¢ Headings: 600-700 (bold)

Line Height Matters üìè

Space between lines of text

Too Tight

This text is cramped and hard to read because the lines are too close together making it difficult to distinguish where one line ends and the next begins.

Just Right

This text is comfortable to read with good spacing between lines that helps guide your eyes smoothly from one line to the next without getting lost.

Guidelines:
‚Ä¢ Headings: 1.2-1.3 (tight)
‚Ä¢ Body text: 1.6-1.8 (comfortable)
‚Ä¢ Small text: 1.8-2.0 (extra space)
